# Product Context: Riviera Residence

## Purpose
A marketing landing page designed to capture pre-registration leads for a luxury residential project.

## User Experience Goals
- Cinematic, slow-reveal experience that conveys luxury
- Easy navigation between sections
- Smooth scrolling with Lenis
- Clear call-to-actions for lead generation
- Mobile-optimized for on-the-go browsing

## Design Philosophy
- Dark, moody aesthetic with gold accents
- Minimal border-radius (4px)
- Large typography for headings
- Generous white space
- Fade-in animations triggered on scroll
